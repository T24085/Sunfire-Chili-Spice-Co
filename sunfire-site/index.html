About Sunfire

Sunfire Chili & Spice Co. started with a dehydrator, a stack of peppers, and a craving for seasonings that actually tasted like real chiles instead of salt and filler.

We blend California chiles, chile de árbol, habanero and sweet peppers to build layers of flavor: smoky, fruity, bright, and spicy. Every batch is made in small runs so the spices stay fresh and vibrant.

What Makes Us Different

  • No anti-caking agents or weird fillers
  • Sun-dried & dehydrated whole peppers
  • Blends tuned for real cooking, not just heat
  • Crafted in small batches

How It’s Made

Every batch of Sunfire starts with whole peppers that are sorted, cleaned, and gently dried to lock in color and flavor.

After dehydrating, we toast and grind in small batches, then let the blend rest so the flavors marry before it’s packed in airtight jars.

  1. Source sun-ripened peppers
  2. Dehydrate & toast for max flavor
  3. Grind and blend in small batches
  4. Pack fresh & label by hand
